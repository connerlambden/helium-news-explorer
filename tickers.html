How to read these cards

Forecast % is the ML model's central price-change estimate over the next 37 trading days. The cone shades the upper- and lower-bound percentages (the model's uncertainty band), widening with time. The bull/bear narratives are generated alongside the numbers — so the text and the number always agree on the same view of the world.

Short-vol candidates have tight forecast bands → option premium-selling structures may be edge. Long-vol candidates have wide bands or asymmetric tails → option premium-buying structures may be edge. The model is wrong all the time; track its calibration with the Brier-grade recipe.
